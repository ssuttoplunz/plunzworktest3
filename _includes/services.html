Strategies

Community Health Worker

Activating existing networks with Community Health Workers to prevent evictions before they start. These wrkers could provide at home assistance, accessing people in need where they need it.

Campaign Strategy

Increasing access to information to assist tenants that are confronted with evictions.

Restorative Solutions

In order to better facilitate tenant/landlord resolution, there needs to be an alternative space to the traditional courtroom. We proposed leveraging vacant spaces within the communities most in need to create new spaces for restorative resolution.

Eviction Memories

Raise awareness of the risks of eviction by collecting the memories of families that have been affected and removed from their homes.
